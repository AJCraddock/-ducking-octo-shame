 Bill game was created to be a "smart game." Smart games are games designed to teach the user something at the completion of the game. Bill Game teaches the player how scripting languages work and how language trees work.

 Bill game was created by: Andre Craddock, Jeffrey Eldridge, Mark Goodwin, and Adrian Jacobs. They are seniors at University of North Carolina at Greensboro in the Computer Science Department.
